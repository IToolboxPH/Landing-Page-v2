## Executive Summary

IToolbox is a technology startup founded in 2019, focused on developing innovative digital solutions across both public and private sectors. Our mission is to leverage AI, IoT, and web technologies to create practical, scalable products that solve real-world problems across all industries requiring urgent technological innovation—including environmental initiatives, educational platforms, agriculture, healthcare, financial services (fintech), logistics, and manufacturing.

### Vision
To become a leading provider of intelligent digital solutions across public and private sectors, empowering organizations and communities through innovation in environmental sustainability, education, agriculture, healthcare, financial services, logistics, and manufacturing.

### Mission
Deliver high-quality, user-friendly digital products that combine cutting-edge AI, IoT, and web technologies with practical applications. We serve all industries requiring urgent technological innovation to drive efficiency, growth, and positive social impact.

## Product Portfolio

### 1. BilMoko - Restaurant Price Aggregator
**Status:** Planning  
**Target Market:** Restaurant owners, food service businesses, consumers  
**Revenue Model:** Subscription (restaurant analytics), Freemium (consumer app)

**Value Proposition:**
- Real-time price comparison across major food chains
- Data-driven pricing recommendations for restaurants
- Consumer savings through price transparency

**Target Launch:** Q2 2026

---

### 2. MedConnect - Healthcare Management Platform
**Status:** Concept / Planning (Project folder created)  
**Target Market:** Clinics, hospitals, medical professionals (Philippines → Global)  
**Revenue Model:** SaaS subscription (₱2,999-9,999/month)

**Value Proposition:**
- Comprehensive EMR, appointment scheduling, and telemedicine
- PhilHealth integration for claims processing
- Affordable pricing for small-medium clinics
- Philippines-first approach leveraging healthcare industry connections
- Data privacy compliant (Philippine Data Privacy Act)

**Strategy:** Leverage friends/relatives in healthcare as early adopters, validate locally before international expansion

**Target Launch:** Q4 2026 (MVP)

**Projected Revenue:** ₱3.5M (Year 1), ₱18M (Year 2), ₱50M (Year 3)

---

### 3. InForMa - Intelligent Forest Monitoring
**Status:** Active Development (GitHub repo exists)

- Forest fire prevention and illegal logging detection

**Target Launch:** Q3 2026

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Subscription Revenue (Primary)**
   - SaaS subscriptions for DigiCool
   - Premium analytics for BilMoko restaurants
   - Enterprise licenses for InForMa

2. **Government Contracts**
   - InForMa deployments for DENR, LGUs
   - Pilot programs and scaling contracts

3. **Freemium Model**
   - Free consumer app (BilMoko) with premium features
   - Basic school accounts (DigiCool) with paid upgrades

4. **Custom Development**
   - Special features for enterprise clients
   - Integration services

### Cost Structure

**Development Costs:**
- Lab infrastructure (already established)
- Cloud hosting (AWS/Azure)
- API and third-party services
- Testing and QA

**Operational Costs:**
- Marketing and sales
- Customer support
- Legal and compliance
- Administrative expenses

**Growth Investments:**
- Team expansion
- Product development
- Market research
- Partnership development
