# IToolbox Store - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**IToolbox Store** is an e-commerce platform specializing in IoT devices, smart home solutions, mobile devices, and computer peripherals for the Philippine and Southeast Asian markets. Launching in Q2 2026, we aim to become the trusted marketplace for technology products with a focus on quality, customer service, and competitive pricing.

### Business Highlights
- **Market**: Philippines (primary), Southeast Asia (expansion)
- **Target**: Home users, SMBs, enterprises, tech enthusiasts
- **Revenue Model**: Product sales, vendor commissions, premium services
- **Launch**: Q2 2026
- **Break-even**: 18-24 months
- **5-year Revenue Target**: ₱50M+ annually

## Revenue Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Direct Product Sales** (85% of revenue)
   - Average margin: 20-30%
   - Target: ₱40M annually (Year 3)

2. **Vendor Commissions** (8% of revenue)
   - Multi-vendor marketplace
   - 10-15% commission per sale
   - Target: ₱4M annually (Year 3)

3. **Premium Memberships** (3% of revenue)
   - ₱499/month per user
   - Target: 500 members (Year 3)
   - Target: ₱3M annually

4. **Services** (4% of revenue)
   - Installation: ₱1M
   - Extended warranties: ₱1M
   - Total: ₱2M annually (Year 3)

### Pricing Strategy

- **Competitive Pricing**: Match or beat major competitors
- **Value Bundles**: Cross-sell related products at discounts
- **Flash Sales**: Weekly promotions for customer acquisition
- **B2B Pricing**: Volume-based discounts (5-20%)

## Operations Plan

### Supply Chain

#### Sourcing Strategy
1. **Direct Imports** (40%)
   - Work with manufacturers
   - Higher margins
   - Quality control

2. **Local Distributors** (50%)
   - Faster delivery
   - Better support
   - Lower MOQs

3. **Dropshipping** (10%)
   - Test new products
   - Low inventory risk
   - Lower margins

#### Inventory Management
- **Initial Focus**: Top 100 SKUs
- **Turnover Target**: 6-8 times/year
- **Safety Stock**: 30-45 days
- **Warehouse**: 3PL partner initially, own by Year 2

### Fulfillment

#### Shipping Partners
- **Metro Manila**: Same-day/next-day (Lalamove, Grab)
- **Nationwide**: LBC, J&T, Ninja Van (2-5 days)
- **International**: DHL, FedEx (enterprise clients)

#### Fulfillment Targets
- **Order Processing**: Within 24 hours
- **Metro Manila**: Next-day delivery
- **Provincial**: 3-5 days delivery
- **Returns**: 7-day return policy

### Technology Infrastructure

- **E-commerce Platform**: Custom React + Node.js
- **Payment Gateway**: Stripe + local options
- **Inventory System**: Real-time tracking
- **CRM**: Customer data & history
- **Analytics**: Sales, customers, products
