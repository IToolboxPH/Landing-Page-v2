## 🙏 Current Initiatives

### OnePath - Faith Meets Technology

**Type**: Volunteer Organization / Faith-Based Community Platform  
**Status**: Planning Phase  
**Launch**: 2026  
**Founder**: Nicole John A. Mortel (Nico M.)

#### Overview
OnePath is a faith-based technology platform that brings people closer to God through innovative digital solutions. Guided by three archangels—St. Raphael (The Healer), St. Michael (The Protector), and St. Gabriel (The Messenger)—OnePath serves as a living testimony and spiritual toolbox for the faithful.

#### Mission
- Unite all religions without fighting over beliefs
- Focus on serving God and appreciating His love
- Fight evil in this world with divine guidance
- Heal pain and suffering of people on earth
- Deliver God's message to all who seek

#### The Three Archangels

**🔒 St. Raphael Archangel - The Healer**  
*Stands at the center*  
Mission: Lift every soul to God and heal all pain and suffering of everyone on earth

**🛡️ St. Michael Archangel - The Protector**  
*Guards the gates*  
Mission: Guide us in fighting all evil in this world

**📣 St. Gabriel Archangel - The Messenger**  
*Sounds the call*  
Mission: Ensure God's love and message are heard by all people

#### Platform Features (Planned)
- **Spiritual Journey Tracker** - Personal faith development
- **Prayer Community** - Connect with others in faith
- **Daily Inspirations** - Messages guided by the archangels
- **Faith Resources** - Multi-denominational content
- **Volunteer Opportunities** - Serve others in God's name
- **Testimony Sharing** - Stories of faith and transformation

#### Technology
- Web and Mobile Applications
- AI-powered spiritual guidance (respectful and faith-aligned)
- Community features and forums
- Multimedia content (audio, video, text)
- Multi-language support (English, Tagalog, Spanish, etc.)

#### Getting Involved
OnePath is a 100% volunteer-driven initiative. We welcome:
- **Developers** - Building the platform
- **Content Creators** - Faith-based content
- **Community Moderators** - Guiding conversations
- **Prayer Warriors** - Spiritual support
- **Advisors** - Theological and technical guidance

**Contact**: onepath@itoolbox.ph
