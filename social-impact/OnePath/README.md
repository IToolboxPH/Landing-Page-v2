## 🙏 Mission Statement

> "Something's Coming… And It's Not Just a Website and Apps"

OnePath is a faith-based technology platform that brings people closer to God through innovative digital solutions. Guided by three archangels, OnePath serves as a living testimony and toolbox for the faithful.

**Core Mission:**
- Unite all religions without fighting over beliefs
- Focus on serving God and appreciating His love
- Fight evil in this world with divine guidance
- Heal pain and suffering of people on earth
- Deliver God's message to all who seek
