## 💰 Funding Strategy

### Bootstrap Phase (2026)
**Zero Cost Approach:**
- Use free hosting platforms
- Free tier of services (Firebase, Netlify)
- Volunteer content creation
- Organic social media growth
- No paid advertising initially

### Future Funding (2027+)
**If Community Grows:**
- Accept voluntary donations via:
  - PayPal/GCash (free accounts)
  - Patreon (for recurring supporters)
  - Ko-fi or Buy Me a Coffee
- **100% Transparency:**
  - Regular financial updates
  - Show how donations are used
  - Always keep core content free
  - Never paywall spiritual resources

**Potential Uses for Donations:**
- Better hosting as traffic grows
- Mobile app development costs
- Content creation (graphics, videos)
- Translation to other languages
- Community events (online gatherings)
